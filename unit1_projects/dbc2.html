Draw Apple's Logo!

Here's a simple way to accurately draw the Apple Logo using a compass and a coordinate plane…

  1. Draw a dot at the origin of the coordinate plane, and label that point C1.
  2. Draw a circle with a radius of 2 inches (2”), and a center at C1.
  3. Draw a circle with a diameter of 2 inches, and a center at the point (0, -16)
  4. Label the center C2.
  5. Draw the same circle as C2, but reflect it over the x-axis. Label the center C3.
  6. Translate the center of C3, 1 unit left and 1 unit up. And draw circle C4, with the same radius.
  7. Translate the center of C4, 5 unit right and 3 units down and draw a circle of the same size. Label it C5.
  8. Shade in the area between C4 and C5.
  9. Draw a circle with a radius of 1 inch (1”), with a center at (-5, 6). Label the center C6.
  10. Reflect C6 over the y-axis and draw another circle of the same size.
  11. Draw a circle with a center of (-10, -2) and a radius of 5/8“. Label the circle C8.
  12. Reflect C8 over the y-axis and draw a circle of the same size.
  13. Draw a circle with a radius of 3/4” and a center that is 5 units right and 6 units down from C8.
  14. Reflect the previous circle over the y-axis and draw a circle congruent to it.
  15. Last circle! With a center at (14, 2) draw a circle with a radius 1 ¼“.
  16. Use the circles as a guide to draw the logo around them! When you’re confident you’ve got it, trace it with a marker and marvel at your beautiful artwork!
